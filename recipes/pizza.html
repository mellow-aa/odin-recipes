How To Make Pizza

Below, you will find the ingredients and steps needed to make the best pizza worthy of the ninja turtles approval!

Ingredients:

  • Oven
  • Box of Pizza(preferably digorno)
  • Patience

Steps:

  1. Take pizza out of box
  2. Preheat Oven
  3. Throw pizza in oven and wait 20 minutes
  4. Enjoy, Cowabunga dude!
