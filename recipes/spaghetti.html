How to Make Spaghetti

Below, you will find all the ingredients and steps needed to make a damn good spaghetti!

Ingredients:

  • Ground Turkey
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Brown the ground Turkey
  2. Pour the tomato sauce into the Browned Turkey
  3. Boil the spaghetti noodles
  4. Pour the spaghetti sauce into the noodles
  5. Spread cheese on the spaghetti
  6. Enjoy!
