How To Make Lasagna

Below, you will find all the ingredients needed to make the most scrumptious lasagna that would leave even garfield speechless.

ingredients:

  • Italian Sausage
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Love

Steps:

  1. Cook the Italian Sausage.
  2. Pour the tomato sauce into the cooked sausage
  3. Boil the Pasta
  4. Place the cooked pasta into a pan
  5. Spread tomato soaked sauage onto pasta
  6. Spread Cheese
  7. bake in over forever
  8. Enjoy!
